Sourdough Bread
Our handcrafted sourdough is made using a traditional natural starter, allowing the dough to ferment and develop its signature depth of flavor. Each loaf takes time, patience, and care, resulting in a beautiful balance of a crisp, caramelized crust and a soft, chewy crumb with a delightful tang. This natural fermentation process not only gives our sourdough its unique taste but also makes it easier to digest, as the slow fermentation helps break down gluten.
