Sourdough Bread
Our handcrafted sourdough is made using a traditional natural starter, allowing the dough to ferment and develop its signature depth of flavor. Each loaf takes time, patience, and care, resulting in a beautiful balance of a crisp, caramelized crust and a soft, chewy crumb with a delightful tang. This natural fermentation process not only gives our sourdough its unique taste but also makes it easier to digest, as the slow fermentation helps break down gluten. It‚Äôs a wholesome choice for those seeking a delicious, all-natural bread experience. Enjoy it fresh from the oven or toast a slice to enjoy with butter, jam, or your favorite savory toppings. Pair it with soups, create the perfect sandwich, or simply enjoy it on its own‚Äîthe versatility of our sourdough makes it a must-have in any kitchen.
